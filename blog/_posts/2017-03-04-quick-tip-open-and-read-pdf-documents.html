WhatsApp is well know messaging platform and recently it had introduced its web version. Using it we can send and receive messages that includes text, images , videos and now PDF Documents.

But the problem was that when we download any PDF Documents it simply downloads the file and we couldn't open that file.


Here is the simple solution to overcome that issue.

Solution #1 :You can simply Right Click -> Open With -> Choose your PDF Reader. Even your Browser can do the task.
But this not a permanent solution, Each time you have to do so.



Solution #2 (Which I Prefer): Just Rename (or press F2) and add a .pdf extension to it. (Or Rename Accordingly)

Voila!!You are done..
