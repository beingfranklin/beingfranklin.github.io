Who are you ?
Personal Domains play a vital role in Personal Branding. They gives a boost to the marketing and branding sector. Many of us would already be knowing it. For those who don't, Its time to rethink.

When we observe  the trends in the blogosphere,we see that giving your blog your name as domain name increases the popularity and helps in Personal branding. Its easy to remember names rather than boring blog titles.

People can fill them with their random blog posts and sometimes they can be even used as an online portfolio .By doing so you present yourself as a brand to the people out there.
 
Many Pros often do keep separate blogs , so as to differentiate themselves from the others. Like John Saddington's john.do ,chris coyier's chriscoyier.com , Amit Agarwal's amitagarwal.com .. and the list goes on.
 

Why should I do it ? What happens if I don't ?

Its simple. You don't lose anything. But you may lose the chance to brand yourself as some Pro’s do. The trends are changing and people are nowadays just exchanging their domain names instead of their business cards .
Stand Out !!.Narrow your Chances.

You lose all the Advantages listed below.
  • Standing Out from the Crowd.It helps you to show people how different and cool you are and as I mentioned above, it comes handy during business conversations.
  • Its the same Always . Whenever the trend changes you don't have to change your domain name as your domain name stays same as long as your name stays.
  • Gives an Idea and Unique Identity about you in the Web. As I mentioned above, uniqueness is the key factor that differentiates ourselves from others
  • Personal Email and other personal sub-domains. You get a bulk of of sub domains and email id’s (as per your hosting provider’s conditions). Having a separate email id and subdomains under our name is always cool !
  • Vulnerability to loose Domain in your name. Yeah that's Right. More than 398Kbabies are born per day (roughly) and who knows they don't have your name.

Things to Keep in Mind Before Choosing Personal Domains


  1. Be Original. Show that its you. Never try to replicate others’ ideas and stuff’s saying that its your own.you must posses some ethics and values .Always you be obliged to it.
  2. Go for .com and .me then others.
     People find it easier to say .com automatically and its the most common one too. If you cant get the right .com domain name ,go for other Top Level Domain names (TLD’s) like .me,.net etc .
  3. Simple and clean.Avoid unwanted punctuations and silly words in between .Keep it neat ,simple and easy to spell.
  4. Not too long. Longer Domain names can be annoying as its not easy to read or figure your name from it.
    (Your First Name)+(Your Middlename )+ (Your Last Name).com would make it unique. But Never do this. Always go for (Your First Name).com or (Your Last Name).com or at certain extend (Your First Name)+(Your Last Name).com  not (Your First Name)+(Your Middlename )+ (Your Last Name).com
