Indian Telecommunication Department has blocked many websites for posting copyright issues. All those popular sites which has served for generations has been come under this ban.




But the interesting thing is that Behance has also been put under this Ban. For People who is new to this , its a social networking site for creative people who shares their work and other creative stuffs. Just like sharing Photos in Instagram, people share their creative arts and other stuffs over here. Its a community from which you can leverage maximum out of it.

Its strange that apart from having such a strong creative community over here. No one has raised their voice for this problem and even Adobe hasn't taken a note of it, i guess.
Whatever its hard when things changes like this.

However we'll see how to bypass those bans by Simply changing your current DNS settings.

Note: Please note down the current DNS settings which may come in handy if you want to revert back your settings

All you have to do is to change your TCP/IP Settings and point your DNS to Google's Public DNS

  • IPv4: 8.8.8.8 and/or 8.8.4.4
  • IPv6: 2001:4860:4860::8888 &/or 2001:4860:4860::8844
You can see full step by step procedure in Google's Developer page over Here.

P.S: This also removes restrictions for some other sites like torrent sites also.
